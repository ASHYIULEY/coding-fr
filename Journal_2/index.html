“...an edgeless surface of unknown proportions comprised of small, individual, and variable elements
from multiple vantages assembled into a readable whole
that documents a moment...”

Frank Chimero challenges us to think out of the box. Literally. However, there's a place for the box.
More like...an "element" that can hold shape like a box–but is not confined to itself.
I personally loved the visual metaphor through David Hockney that Chimero creates to demonstrate clarity amongst "chaos."
From it, I was inspired to create something similar; different photos to create one that looks same, communicating cultural sameness.
